THEME
Disease

SUBTHEME
Impacts of Waterborne and vector diseases

PROBLEM STATEMENT
A survey of the impacts of waterborne and vector diseases on the residents of Georgetown's Stabroek neighbourhood, Guyana.

RESEARCH QUESTIONS
1. What kinds of diseases are found in the neighbourhood of Stabroek?
2. What causes these diseases?
3. What effects do those diseases have on residents?
4. What are some solutions to the disease problem in the neighbourhood of Stabroek?
